About Us

"AQUADUCO WATER INFRASTRUCTURE TREATMENT" provides construction services within the component of water infrastructure. The company is based in the United Arab Emirates and operates from its head office in Dubai.


Having a strong engineering and planning team "AQUADUCO WATER INFRASTRUCTURE TREATMENT" is capable of commencing work on relevant projects around the globe.

Our Services

Planning and Engineering

We approach each project individually and propose the best design solutions.

Procurement

Having a vast network of suppliers, our company is capable of procuring all the required elements for the project works.

Civil Works

By implementing strong requirements for the selection of sub-contractors, we are enabled for maintaining projects abroad. All management procedures are handled directly by "AQUADUCO WATER INFRASTRUCTURE TREATMENT"

Installation

We complete projects on a turnkey basis, which includes installation of temporary facilities, fabrication yards, Installation of metal structures and technological pipelines; Hot galvanizing of metal structures; Anticorrosive protection of surfaces; Thermal insulation works; Argon welding; Installation of equipment; Arrangement of concrete and reinforced concrete monolithic structures.
